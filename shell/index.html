Shell Shockers

Shell Shockers (Shellshock.io) is a multiplayer .io FPS game featuring eggs armed with guns. You control one of these weapon-wielding eggs in one of four online game modes where the aim is to shatter your opponents with bullets and bombs. It's the ultimate online egg shooting game! Jump into the game and select your character name, customize your egg, and choose one of four game modes. In this game, you must work hard to protect yourself and dish out damage to opposing eggs. Try out each game mode and see if you can conquer the Shell Shockers arena.
